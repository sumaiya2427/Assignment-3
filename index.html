Frequently Asked Questions

What are the health benefits of eating vegetables?

Vegetables are rich in essential nutrients, including vitamins, minerals, fiber, and antioxidants. They help improve digestion, boost immunity, lower the risk of chronic diseases, and support overall health.

How many servings of vegetables should I eat daily?

The recommended daily intake varies based on age and activity level, but generally, adults should eat at least 2.5 to 3 cups of vegetables per day.

What are some good sources of fiber among vegetables?

High-fiber vegetables include broccoli, carrots, spinach, peas, Brussels sprouts, and sweet potatoes
